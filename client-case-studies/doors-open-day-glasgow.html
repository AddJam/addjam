Lets Celebrate Glasgow!

Add Jam created Glasgow Walking, a mobile app to uncover heritage information in our home city, as part of the Innovate UK Future City Project. through development an opportunity came up to transform the existing Glasgow Walking app into a information app for the annual Doors Open Day event in the city.

Doors Open Day is a free festival that celebrates Glasgowâ€™s buildings, streets, parks, architecture, history and people with a programme full of walks, tours, talks, events and more than 100 buildings open for anyone to visit. For one week only, doors are opened and you are encouraged to explore Glasgow in a new way.

Easy to digest information on our heritage

The Doors Open Day Glasgow app includes information such as opening and closing times, on site amenities for each attraction and detailed information on the history of each building.

The app is a native iOS app and uses local storage to ensure participants in Doors Open Day can engage with the app even in the most remote parts of the city or deepest bunkers.

Being based on the Glasgow Walking code base, both the web and mobile portions of Doors Open Day Glasgow borrows many UI elements from Glasgow Walking. Examples of this is such as the compass on lists and extensive use of Blur views. The Doors Open Day Glasgow app was however heavily customised to suit the needs of Glasgow Building Preservation Trust.
