Doors Open Day Glasgow

The key to the city is yours. Happy exploring.

Lets Celebrate Glasgow!

Add Jam created Glasgow Walking, a mobile app to uncover heritage information in our home city, as part of the Innovate UK Future City Project. through development an opportunity came up to transform the existing Glasgow Walking app into a information app for the annual Doors Open Day event in the city.

Doors Open Day is afree festival that celebrates Glasgowâ€™s buildings, streets, parks, architecture, history and people with a programme full of walks, tours, talks, events and more than 100 buildings open for anyone to visit. For one week only, doors are opened and you are encouraged to explore Glasgow in a new way.

Easy to digest information on our heritage

The app is on iPhones and includes information such as opening and closing times, on site ammenities for each attraction and detailed information on the history of each building.

The app is a native iOS app and uses local storage to ensure participants in Doors Open Day can enage with the app even in the most remote parts of the city or deepest bunkers.

The app borrows UI elements from Glasgow Walking such as the compass on lists and exentisve use of Blur views. The app was however heavily customised to suit the needs of Glasgow Building Preservation Trust
